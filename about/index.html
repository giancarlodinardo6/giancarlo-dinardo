Background

I’m a senior paid social strategist with 9+ years focused on Meta advertising across lead generation and eCommerce. My work has always lived between performance and creative: reading account behavior, finding the actual constraint, and turning that into better tests, briefs, and decisions.

I’ve worked both in-house and at agencies, with ownership over performance across Facebook and Instagram campaigns. The throughline has been learning how creative, campaign structure, offer, and audience signals interact once real spend is involved.

This site is my digital home for that thinking. The case studies show how I approached real account problems. The playbooks and systems show how I turn recurring decisions into frameworks that can be reused.

What I actually do

Most of my work is diagnosing why an account is underperforming and building the structure to fix it. That usually means looking at creative testing logic, campaign architecture, message-market fit, and lifecycle stage before assuming the answer is more spend or more ads.

I’m especially interested in the creative side of performance: how hooks select buyers, how coverage expands the market, and how winning ads evolve over time instead of becoming one-off dependencies.
