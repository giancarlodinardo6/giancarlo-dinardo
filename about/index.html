About me

Ten years on Meta, spent learning what keeps working.

I'm Giancarlo, a paid social and creative strategist. I've spent the last decade running Meta accounts across lead gen and eCommerce, in-house and on the agency side, long enough to stop trusting one-off wins and start building the systems that make results repeatable.

The work I care about sits where creative strategy and media buying are the same job, not two handoffs. A winning ad is a starting point. The real work is understanding why it won and building a method that produces more of them, then giving the account the architecture to scale without frequency becoming a ceiling.
