Background

I come from paid social, but the work has always sat between performance and creative. I spend most of my time using performance data to guide what creative gets tested next and how it evolves over time.

I've worked both in-house and at agencies, across eCommerce and lead generation, with full ownership over performance. Nine years running Meta campaigns, Facebook and Instagram, managing seven figures in ad spend and working closely across research, creative direction, and execution.

More recently, I've been formalizing this way of working into AI-powered systems that help speed up research, testing, and creative production, while keeping decisions grounded in experience.

What I actually do

Most of my work is diagnosing why an account is underperforming and building the structure to fix it. That usually means looking at creative testing logic, campaign architecture, and message-market fit before touching any budget.

The case studies on this site document that process across nine real accounts. The playbooks and frameworks are how I've systematized the recurring decisions so they don't have to be rebuilt from scratch each time.
